What this system does:
-Equipment management
Easily keep track of all your field equipment — like pumps, compressors, valves, and generators — in one place. You can store details such as location, serial numbers, type, and current status.

-Maintenance requests
Create and manage maintenance tasks for any piece of equipment. Each request follows a clear workflow (Draft → Confirmed → In Progress → Done), so you always know what’s happening and what needs attention.

-Technician management
Manage your technicians more effectively by extending Odoo’s contact system. You can add useful details like their specialization and employee ID to keep everything organized.

-User roles & permissions
Control access with two simple roles: Technicians can view and update records, while Managers have full control over the system.

-Activity tracking (Chatter)
Stay on top of everything with built-in communication tools — log messages, track updates, and schedule activities directly within each record.
